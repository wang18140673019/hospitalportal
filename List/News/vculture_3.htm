社会贡献

 

 

 

1949年至1950年，我院组建血吸虫病防治队奔赴嘉定、青浦、宝山开展血吸虫防病治病工作

From 1949 to 1950, we organized Anti-schistosomiasis teams and went to JIading, Qinpu and Baoshan country to work on the examination, trentment and prevention of schistosomiasis

 

1950年，安徽水灾，我院派出医疗队奔赴皖北防汛救灾

In 1950, flood happened in Anhui, we sent medical team to north Anhui to fight the flood

 

 

1951年至1955年，我院先后派出7批34人医疗队奔赴抗美援朝第一线，其中19人立功获奖

From January 1951 to July 1955, we sent 34 persons altogether in 7 batches to participate in movement to resist U.S.aggression and aid Korea, among which 19 persons made meritorious service and won award

 

1954年，安徽水灾，我院派出医疗队奔赴芜湖地区防汛救灾

In 1954, flood happened in Anhui, we sent medical teams to Whhu district to fight the flood

 

1965年至2009年，我院先后派出20批援外医疗队，奔赴索马里、阿尔及利亚、多哥、柬埔寨、摩洛哥等国执行援外医疗任务

From 1965 to 2009, we escorted 20 medical teams successively to developing countries such as Somalia, Algeria, Togo, Cambolia and Morocco to provide medical aids

 

 

1976年，河北唐山大地震，我院派出3批医疗队奔赴唐山抗震救灾

In 1976, massive earthquake happened in Tangshan, Heibei Province, we sent 4 batches of medical teams to help disaster relief
